WHO WE ARE

All our customers receive our FREE "bottomless" spot cleaner, shoe protectors, extra services including pre-vacuuming when necessary, no extra charge spot treating, post cleaning rinse and grooming (if desired) for cut pile carpeting. In addition, we answer our phones 24-hours-a-day-7 days a week for emergency water removal and drying services. We are totally committed to your complete satisfaction.
