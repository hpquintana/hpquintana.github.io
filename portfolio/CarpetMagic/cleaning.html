OUR SERVICES

All our customers receive our FREE "bottomless" spot cleaner, shoe protectors, extra services including pre-vacuuming when necessary, no extra charge spot treating, post cleaning rinse and grooming (if desired) for cut pile carpeting. We provide 6-day-a-week carpet, upholstery, tile and air duct cleaning services. In addition, we answer our phones 24-hours-a-day-7 days a week for emergency water removal and drying services. We are totally committed to your complete satisfaction. If you are ever dissatisfied with any of our services, please feel free to call. We appreciate your business and your confidence in us.

STANDARD CLEANING

Perfect for those on a budget

Our "Standard" full service cleaning includes the pre-vacuuming of your carpets to remove most dry soil before we begin, the pre-treating of all your stains and traffic areas to loosen the sugars and oils on those stubborn areas, the cleaning which is all hot water extraction (more commonly called "steam cleaning") from truck mounted units ensuring a powerful and deep cleaning, a post cleaning fiber rinse (this step is important because it is like a fabric softener for your carpet removing any residual left behind and neutralizing the PH in your carpet which will keep it cleaner longer) and a thorough grooming of your cut pile* carpet to lift the pile and reduce drying times.

Dri-Magic RX 20 Heavy Duty

A quick dry experience defeating even the toughest stains

Our Dri-Magic service is still hot water extraction, but the RX-20 HE unit performs 10 cleaning passes every second providing a heavy duty cleaning while leaving 6 times less moisture behind so you can resume normal activity more quickly after your Dri-Magic cleaning is completed.
